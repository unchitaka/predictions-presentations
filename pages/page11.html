What the output means

The output is a monthly series for each target group (e.g., cause/L6).

  • Predicted complaints (monthly)
  • Machines in field (by cohort aging + production forecast)
  • Implied failure rate (from the aging curve + scaling)

Some systems also prorate monthly to daily, but the core forecast is monthly.
