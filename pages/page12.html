Limits (short but explicit)

Predictions can be wrong for simple reasons:

  • Thin or biased history → weak forecast
  • Wrong CM dates / effects → wrong result
  • Future differs from past in ways not modeled

The model is most useful when used as a decision aid: compare scenarios (CM earlier/later, EOL yes/no).
